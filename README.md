# 💫 About Me:
👋 Hi, I'm Engels Almanzar<br><br>🎓 I am a Cybersecurity student at ITLA, currently one year away from graduation. My passion for technology started at a young age, and before joining ITLA, I developed my cybersecurity skills through self-study, online courses, practical labs, and Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges.<br><br>🛡️ I have practical experience in cybersecurity operations, including security monitoring, SIEM alert analysis, incident investigation, threat monitoring, and basic threat hunting. I have also participated in digital forensics activities and worked with tools such as Wireshark, Nmap, Burp Suite, Tenable, VMware, Docker, and Belkasoft X.<br><br>🚩 One of my favorite areas is cybersecurity training and CTF development. I have created and supported six CTF competitions and technical labs for students, private organizations, and members of the National Police of the Dominican Republic. These challenges covered topics such as digital forensics, web security, OSINT, Linux, Windows, and incident response.<br><br>💻 I regularly practice on platforms like TryHackMe and Hack The Box to strengthen my technical skills and stay up to date with cybersecurity trends. I enjoy solving problems, learning new technologies, and applying knowledge through hands-on projects.<br><br>🌱 Currently learning more about SOC Operations, Incident Response, Threat Hunting, Malware Analysis, Digital Forensics, Cloud Security, and Cyber Defense.<br><br> 📫 Feel free to connect with me and collaborate on cybersecurity projects.
